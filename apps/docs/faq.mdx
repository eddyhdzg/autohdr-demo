# Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is AutoHDR?">
    AutoHDR is an AI-powered photo editing service for real estate.
    Upload your photos and receive professionally enhanced images with
    HDR, sky replacement, object removal, and more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What services does AutoHDR offer?">
    HDR editing, flambient editing, day-to-dusk conversion, grass greening,
    object removal, virtual staging, and TV blackout.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How fast is the turnaround?">
    Most photos are delivered within minutes on Pro and Enterprise plans.
    The free tier has 24-hour delivery.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does pricing work?">
    You only pay for what you download. Start with 10 free photos per month,
    then scale up with volume-based pricing starting at $0.57/photo.
    You can top up credits at your plan rate anytime.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I integrate AutoHDR with my workflow?">
    Yes. AutoHDR integrates with Spiro, HD PhotoHub, Tonomo, Aryeo,
    and Dropbox for seamless, automated delivery.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
    Yes. Get up to 10 free photo downloads per month with standard HDR
    processing. No credit card required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats are supported?">
    AutoHDR accepts JPG files up to 20 MB each. Edited photos are
    delivered as high-resolution JPGs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upload unlimited photos?">
    Yes. Upload as many photos as you want. AutoHDR only charges you
    for the photos you choose to download.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused credits roll over?">
    On Pro plans, yes. Unused credits roll over month-to-month.
    Free plan credits reset each month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I buy extra credits beyond my plan?">
    Yes. On any plan, you can purchase additional credits at your plan's
    per-photo rate. For example, a Basic plan user pays $0.57/photo for
    extra credits â€” the same rate as their plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
