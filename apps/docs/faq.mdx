## Uploading & Processing Photos

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I upload photos to AutoHDR?">
    1. Go to [AutoHDR](https://autohdr.vercel.app)
    2. Drag and drop your files or upload from your computer/Dropbox/Drive
    3. AutoHDR automatically detects and processes your images.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats does AutoHDR accept?">
    - **JPEG** (Recommended for the best speed & performance).
    - **RAW** (Supported, but slower to process).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I upload an unsupported file?">
    Convert to JPEG before processing for the best results.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does AutoHDR automate uploads and delivery?">
    Yes! AutoHDR can automatically detect, edit, and upload photos to your
    delivery platform. Available via several one-click automations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recommended Shooting Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the best settings for AutoHDR?">
    - 3 or 5 bracketed exposures at +/-2 EV.
    - JPEG format recommended (faster uploads, no quality loss in most cases).
    - Tripod is NOT required.
    - Including flash shots ("Flambient") is supported but not needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Team & Collaboration Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can teams use AutoHDR together?">
    Yes! You can collaborate with your team by adding photographers and editors
    to your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I set up my team?">

</AccordionGroup>

## Editing & Customization Tools

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I adjust my images after AutoHDR processes them?">
    Once your images are ready, you can use:
    - **AutoRemove** — One-click object removal (no Photoshop needed).
    - **AutoFill** — Add fireplace flames, flowers, or minor staging edits.
    - **Virtual Twilight** — Converts daytime shots into twilight images.
    - **Adjust Sliders** — Fine-tune brightness, contrast, white balance, vibrance, etc.
    - **Crop Tool** — Adjust framing if needed.
    - **Sky Toggle** — Turn clouds on/off (in case of odd formations).
    - **Green Grass** — Replace the lawn with AI generated grass.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add my own human editor?">
    Yes! AutoHDR has a one-click "Human-in-the-Loop" feature.
    - You can add your own editor for free — no extra cost.
    - They can tweak images before downloading.
    - All images you request a human edit for will be sent to your editor.
    - In one click, they can download the RAWs for that photo, drop the replacement
      image into an upload box, and it will swap the original image automatically in
      your gallery.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
