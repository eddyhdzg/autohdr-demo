# Changelog

All notable updates to AutoHDR are listed here. Check back regularly to see what's new.

---

## March 2026

### New: Virtual Staging v2

Enhanced AI staging with more furniture styles and room types. You can now stage kitchens, home offices, and outdoor patios in addition to living rooms and bedrooms.

[Learn more about Virtual Staging →](/features/virtual-staging)

### Improved: Faster HDR delivery

Average delivery time reduced from 3 minutes to under 90 seconds. You get your edited photos back faster so you can deliver to clients sooner.

### Fixed: Dropbox sync reliability

Resolved intermittent sync failures for large shoot uploads via Dropbox. Your photos now sync consistently, even for shoots with 200+ photos.

[Learn more about the Dropbox integration →](/integrations/dropbox)

---

## February 2026

### New: Day-to-Dusk batch processing

You can now apply Day-to-Dusk editing to an entire shoot at once. Select all your exterior photos and convert them in a single batch.

[Learn more about Day-to-Dusk →](/features/day-to-dusk)

### Improved: Object Removal accuracy

Improved AI detection for common real estate clutter like trash cans, garden hoses, and parked cars. Fewer manual touchups needed.

[Learn more about Object Removal →](/features/object-removal)

### Fixed: Credit balance display

Fixed an issue where your credit balance did not update immediately after purchasing a new credits pack. Your balance now refreshes in real time.
