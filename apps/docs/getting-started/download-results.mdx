# Download Your Results

After AutoHDR processes your photos, you can review them side-by-side
and download the ones you're happy with.

## Delivery times

| Plan       | Typical delivery   |
| ---------- | ------------------ |
| Free       | Up to 24 hours     |
| Pro        | Minutes            |
| Enterprise | Priority processing |

## Review your edits

1. Open your project in the Studio
2. Use the **side-by-side comparison** to see before and after
3. Select the photos you want to download
4. Click **Download**

<Info>
  You're only charged for photos you download. Upload and preview as
  many as you like at no cost.
</Info>

## Bulk download

For large shoots, use the **Select All** option to download your
entire project at once.
