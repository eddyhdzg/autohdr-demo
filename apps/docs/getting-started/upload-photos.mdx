# Upload Your Photos

AutoHDR accepts photos through the Studio web interface or via integrations
like Dropbox and Spiro.

<Snippet file="photo-requirements.mdx" />

## Upload via Studio

1. Log in to your AutoHDR account
2. Open the **Studio**
3. Drag and drop your photos, or click **Upload** to browse your computer
4. AutoHDR automatically detects bracketed exposures and groups them

<Tip>
  You can upload as many photos as you want. AutoHDR only charges you for
  the photos you choose to download.
</Tip>

## Upload via Dropbox

If you've connected Dropbox, simply save your photos to your designated
AutoHDR folder. They'll be automatically picked up and processed.

See [Dropbox Integration](/integrations/dropbox) for setup instructions.
