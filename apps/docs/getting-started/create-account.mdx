# Create Your Account

AutoHDR offers a free plan with 10 photo downloads per month. No credit card required.

## Sign up

1. Visit [AutoHDR](https://autohdr.vercel.app)
2. Click **Get Started Free**
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. You're ready to upload your first photos

<Info>
  Your free plan includes standard HDR processing with 24-hour delivery.
  Upgrade to Pro for priority processing (most photos in minutes).
</Info>

## What you get on the free plan

| Feature             | Free Plan    |

| Monthly free photos | 10           |
| HDR processing      | Standard     |
| Delivery time       | 24 hours     |
| Pay-as-you-go rate  | $0.80/photo  |
| Upload limit        | Unlimited    |
