## How Credits Work

Your plan includes a set number of credits each month. Here's the credit lifecycle:

1. **Earn** — Your plan includes a set number of credits each month (e.g., 50 on Basic).
2. **Use** — One credit = one downloaded photo. Uploading and previewing are always free.
3. **Rollover** — Unused credits on any paid plan roll over month-to-month. No wasted credits.
4. **Top up** — Need more? Buy extra credits at your plan's per-photo rate. No need to upgrade plans.
5. **Expiry** — If you cancel or downgrade to the Free plan, remaining credits expire at the end of your current billing cycle.

<Info>
  **Example:** On the Basic plan (\$0.57/photo), you get 50 credits/month.
  If you need 120 photos one month, buy 70 extra at \$0.57 each —
  the same rate as your plan, not the Free tier rate of \$0.80.
</Info>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do unused credits expire?">
    On paid plans, unused credits roll over month-to-month — they never expire
    as long as you stay on a paid plan. Free plan credits reset each month.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I downgrade and keep my credits?">
    If you downgrade to a lower paid plan, your rollover credits carry over.
    However, if you cancel or downgrade to the Free plan, credits expire at the
    end of your current billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When am I charged a credit?">
    Only when you click Download. Uploading and previewing are always free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my plan?">
    You can buy extra credits at your plan's per-photo rate at any time.
    There's no need to upgrade to a higher plan — just top up and keep going.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does yearly billing work?">
    Pay upfront for 12 months and save on every tier. The discount varies
    by plan — for example, the Basic plan drops from \$28.50/mo to \$22.80/mo
    (a \$5.70/mo savings). Higher tiers save even more.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
