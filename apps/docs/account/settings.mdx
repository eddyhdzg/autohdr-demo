# Account Settings

Manage your AutoHDR account from the Settings page in the Studio.

## Profile

Update your name, email, and profile photo. Your Google sign-in email
is your primary account identifier.

## Notification preferences

Choose how you want to be notified when your photos are ready:

- Email notifications
- In-app notifications

## Connected integrations

View and manage your connected services (Dropbox, Spiro, etc.)
from the Integrations tab in Settings.
