# Billing

Manage your subscription and payment details from the Billing page.

## Change your plan

Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect
at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Payment methods

AutoHDR accepts major credit cards and debit cards.

## Invoices

Download invoices for any billing period from the Billing page.

## Cancel your subscription

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll keep
access to your current plan until the end of the billing period,
then revert to the Free plan.
