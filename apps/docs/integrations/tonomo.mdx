# Tonomo Integration

Connect AutoHDR with Tonomo to streamline your photo editing workflow.

## Setup

Contact [delivery@autohdr.com](mailto:delivery@autohdr.com) for integration
setup instructions specific to your Tonomo account.

## How it works

Once configured, Tonomo sends your photos to AutoHDR for processing.
Edited photos are delivered back to your Tonomo project automatically.
