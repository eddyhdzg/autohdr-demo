# Aryeo Integration

Connect AutoHDR with Aryeo to streamline your photo editing workflow.

## Setup

Contact [delivery@autohdr.com](mailto:delivery@autohdr.com) for integration
setup instructions specific to your Aryeo account.

## How it works

Once configured, Aryeo sends your photos to AutoHDR for processing.
Edited photos are delivered back to your Aryeo project automatically.
