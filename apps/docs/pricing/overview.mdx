# Pricing

AutoHDR uses a simple credit-based pricing model.
**You only pay for photos you download.**

Upload as many photos as you want. Preview them, compare side-by-side,
and only pay when you're ready to download.

## Plans at a glance

|                    | Free      | Pro           | Enterprise |
| ------------------ | --------- | ------------- | ---------- |
| Monthly free photos | 10        | 50-10,000     | Custom     |
| Per-photo cost     | $0.80     | $0.57-$0.40   | Custom     |
| Delivery           | 24 hours  | Minutes       | Priority   |
| Rollover credits   | No        | Yes           | Yes        |
| Dedicated support  | No        | Slack (higher tiers) | Yes  |

## Key pricing principles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Upload unlimited, pay only for downloads">
    Upload and preview as many photos as you want. You're only charged
    when you download an edited photo.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Top up credits at your plan rate">
    On any paid plan, you can purchase additional credits at the same
    per-photo rate as your plan. A Basic plan user buying extra credits
    still pays $0.57/photo, not the free tier rate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Save 20% with annual billing">
    Switch to yearly billing and save 20% on any Pro plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
